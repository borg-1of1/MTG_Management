### **Moxfield Deck Organization Guide (Integrated Master Edition)**

This guide establishes a **platform-native, engineer-friendly system** for managing your Magic: The Gathering collection. It prioritizes clarity by encoding metadata into deck names, descriptions, and comments while aligning physical storage with digital records.

---

#### **1️⃣ Folder Layout (The Lifecycle Stages)**

Moxfield folders provide **coarse grouping**. Use this nested structure to keep the sidebar clean and distinguish between playable decks, active projects, and research.

* **📂 ACTIVE (The Core)**  
  * **EdhDecks:** Built, playable custom and modified Commander decks.  
  * **Duel:** Format-specific decks using the same prefixes and logic.  
  * **Family:** Sub-folders for **Ree, Liili,** and **Cha** (kids' decks).  
* **📂 WORKSHOP (The Pipeline)**  
  * **Planning:** Specifically for **\[S0\] Concept** and **\[S1\] Partial** decks.  
  * **Precon Stock:** Inventory of unmodified decks waiting for processing.  
* **📂 LIBRARY (Reference & Research)**  
  * **Tools:** **(NEW)** For functional reference lists (e.g., "Game Changers" or "Land Fixing").  
  * **NetDecks:** External lists found for research or inspiration.  
  * **Virtual:** Finished designs that are virtual-only (\[S0\]) and not slated for physical assembly.  
  * **Holders:** Sub-folders for **LF, Hordes,** and **FF**.  
* **📂 ARCHIVE (History)**  
  * **Retired:** For **\[S3\] Retired** decks kept for historical records.

---

#### **2️⃣ Deck Name Prefixes (The State)**

Every project deck must have **exactly one Status prefix** in its name.

* **\[S0\] Concept:** Design or virtual-only phase.  
* **\[S1\] Partial:** Missing cards, cards in other decks, or **awaiting physical assembly**.  
* **\[S2\] Built:** Sleeved, playable, and stable.  
* **\[S3\] Retired:** Archived or dismantled but kept visible for records.  
* **\[T\] Testing (Modifier):** A transient tag added when a deck is **under evaluation** (e.g., `[S2][T] Deck Name`).

---

#### **3️⃣ Reference Tools & Static Lists (The Exception)**

Decks located in **LIBRARY/Tools** serve as data repositories rather than project builds. These are the **only exception** to the lifecycle rules.

* **No Status Prefix:** Since these are not "built" or "retired," they do not require an \[S\*\] tag.  
* **Optional \[REF\] Tag:** You may use **\[REF\]** (e.g., `[REF] Game Changers`) for consistent visual alignment.  
* **Purpose:** Use these for checklists (like the WotC Game Changers list) or planning resources (like Land Fixing lists) to see what you own versus what you need.

---

#### **4️⃣ Precon Lineage (The Origin)**

Record the deck's origin at the **top of the description**.

* **Origin: Precon (Stock)** — 0 cards changed from the original list.  
* **Origin: Precon (Polished)** — 1–10 cards replaced (e.g., land base/efficiency swaps).  
* **Origin: Precon (Modified)** — 11–25 cards replaced; core strategy remains.  
* **Origin: Precon (Reimagined)** — 25+ cards replaced **OR** a change in the Commander.  
* **Origin: Custom Build** — Built from scratch.

#### **6️⃣ Card-Level Tagging & Physical Storage**

* **The \#Stock Tag:** For cards removed from a precon and moved to "Considering" to identify the "original DNA."  
* **The \#ProxyLibrary Tag:** Identifies cards physically located in the central binder. **Only apply this tag once the physical card is placed in the binder.**  
* **Assembled Location:** Cards in the mainboard of an **\[S2\]** deck are physically located in that deck's box.

---

#### **7️⃣ Maintenance & Summary Checklist**

* **Upgrades Pending:** Keep as **\[S2\]** in **ACTIVE**. Use a **CHANGELOG comment** to note future goals.  
* **Log Activity:** Use **Moxfield comments** as an **append-only ledger** for major decisions.  
* **Checklist (30–60 Seconds):**  
  1. Confirm Prefix (\[S\*\]) matches the physical state (Built vs. Partial/Concept).  
  2. Verify Origin matches the swap threshold.  
  3. Apply **\#ProxyLibrary** tag if the card has been pulled.  
  4. Add a **CHANGELOG comment** for any state transitions.

## Theme & Strategy

A Fallout-themed proliferate and counters deck built around The Wise Mothman's ability to distribute rad counters and +1/+1 counters across the board. The primary game plan is to use proliferate triggers to scale counters exponentially while milling opponents as a secondary win condition. The deck wins through overwhelming board presence from counter-scaled creatures and incidental mill damage.

---

## Win Conditions

- **Primary**: Combat damage from counter-scaled creatures
- **Secondary**: Mill via Syr Konrad, the Grim and Mindcrank — draining opponents as creatures are milled and leave graveyards
- **Tertiary**: Insidious Roots token generation creating a wide board from graveyard recursion

## Theme & Strategy

A Fallout-themed proliferate and counters deck built around The Wise Mothman's ability to
distribute rad counters and +1/+1 counters across the board. The primary game plan is to
use proliferate triggers to scale counters exponentially while milling opponents as a
secondary win condition. The deck wins through overwhelming board presence from
counter-scaled creatures and incidental mill damage.

---

## Win Conditions

- **Primary**: Combat damage from counter-scaled creatures
- **Secondary**: Mill via Syr Konrad, the Grim and Mindcrank — draining opponents as creatures
  are milled and leave graveyards
- **Tertiary**: Insidious Roots token generation creating a wide board from graveyard recursion

## Resolved Questions

- Syr Konrad + Mindcrank: Confirmed Bracket 3 legal. Not an infinite combo — it terminates
  when libraries are exhausted or opponents are eliminated. However the "each opponent"
  scope of Konrad and "each player" scope of Mindcrank *can* make this engine genuinely
  self-sustaining in multiplayer: one creature milled generates Konrad triggers against
  all opponents, each triggering Mindcrank on a different player, feeding the loop.
  In a four-player game this can close the table in one or two trigger cycles once it
  reaches critical mass. Neither card is on the Game Changers list. Bracket 3 legal,
  but disclose under Rule Zero at bracket-mixed tables.
